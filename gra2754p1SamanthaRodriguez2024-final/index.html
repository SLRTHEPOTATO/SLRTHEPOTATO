Chicken Fajitas

Steps

Gather all ingredients: sliced chicken, peppers, and onions.

Season the chicken with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.

Cook the seasoned chicken in olive oil until browned.

Sauté bell peppers and onions in the same skillet.

Combine the chicken with sautéed vegetables.

Serve the fajitas in warm tortillas with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Sliced Chicken
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Tortillas
  • Your Favorite Toppings
